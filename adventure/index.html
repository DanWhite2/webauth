Universal Orlando Day 1

December 28-29th

We decided to go later in the day to avoid super long lines in the heat. Universal Orlando was amazing with my favorite part being Harry Potter World. We went explored all around that section, which looked like two almost functioning towns. All around the park was an amazing experience with most of the rides being amazing and beautifully decorated. I really recommend the Hagrid, Gringotts, and Velocicoster rides there exhilerating.
